4. Refund & Return Policy

We strive to ensure you are fully satisfied with your purchase. If a valid issue arises (such as receiving a damaged or incorrect item), you may request a refund subject to the highly specific conditions detailed below.

Customized Products: If you order a customized or personalized product, NO refund will be issued under any circumstances. Customized items cannot be resold to others, making their acceptance a mandatory condition of purchase. By ordering a custom product, you agree to this strict no-refund policy.

Important Cost Deduction Rule: If an order is canceled or returned, our store incurs operational costs. Therefore, packaging costs and delivery charges will be deducted from the total paid amount before ANY refund is calculated. This deduction applies strictly to ALL three (3) refund options listed below.

After deducting the packaging and delivery costs, the remaining eligible balance can be refunded via one of the following methods:

  • Option 1 - Store Voucher: You will receive the remaining balance as a digital store voucher, plus an additional bonus of up to 5% (maximum of 150 BDT bonus) on top of the remaining balance. This voucher can be used for future purchases.
  • Option 2 - Full Cash Refund: You will receive a direct cash refund. However, for a cash refund, you will receive exactly 90% of the remaining balance (after the packaging and delivery costs have already been deducted).
  • Option 3 - 50/50 System: You will receive a mixed refund of the remaining balance. 50% will be sent to you as cash, and the other 50% will be provided as a store voucher.
